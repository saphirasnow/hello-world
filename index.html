Hi, I'm Bella! Right now, I'm studying computer science at Carnegie Mellon University with intention to minor in Software Engineering and Computer Graphics. I love solving problems and taking on challenging new projects in CS.

My current focus is on program analysis. I began research this past spring to explore mutation-guided fuzz testing in CMU's PASTA Lab with Dr. Rohan Padhye, and plan to expand on that this fall. Also, for the past two summers, I've worked with the Reference Design, Security Controls, and Architecture (REDSCAR) team at Salesforce to develop automated security bug catching in the form of tools now used across Salesforce.

Additionally, my interest in graphics has led me to serve as a student volunteer with SIGGRAPH doing quality assurance tests for the VR theater as well as closed captioning. My personal projects also include animations as well as programming effects and transitions for my anime music videos (AMVs).

I can be reached at ilaybour@andrew.cmu.edu.
