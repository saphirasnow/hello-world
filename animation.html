Animation

(plus, check out my game design work here!)

Burnt Sugar (2022)

A retelling of classic fairytale Hansel and Gretel using stock images. Created using Adobe After Effects for (Im)Possible Worlds: 2D Animation, Motion Graphics and Visual Effects.

PIE (2022)

An explainer video for the Principle of Inclusion-Exclusion in mathematics. Created using Adobe After Effects for (Im)Possible Worlds: 2D Animation, Motion Graphics and Visual Effects.

School of Computer Science (2022)

An exploration of some of the notable historical events and inventions of Carnegie Mellon University's School of Computer Science. Created using Adobe After Effects and Autodesk Maya for (Im)Possible Worlds: 2D Animation, Motion Graphics and Visual Effects.

After the Door Closes (2020)

A chair puts a book away in a bookshelf. Created using Autodesk Maya. For Introduction to 3D Animation, with attention to rigging, animation, and some sound design.

Pixel (2016)

Pixel, the Stanford OHS mascot, doing a backflip. Created using Autodesk Maya. This project was my introduction to animation, with attention to bending of the legs and arms as well as the physical mechanics of the jump itself.
