Hi, I'm Bella! Right now, I'm studying computer science at Carnegie Mellon University with intention to minor in Software Engineering and Animation and Special Effects. I love solving problems and taking on challenging new projects in CS. Outside of my studies, I enjoy video editing as well as reading and writing fantasy and science fiction.

Besides designing and programming transitions for my own anime music videos (AMVs), I have edited production videos for the Sitka Fine Arts Camp and taught coding and video editing to middle-school students at the Sitka Public Library during past summers. My current projects include working on a biometric shirt for patients with Dravet Syndrome and a program for achieving chroma key effects using an arbitrary background.

I can be reached at ilaybour@andrew.cmu.edu.
